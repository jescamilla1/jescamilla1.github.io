About

I'm slow to start on anything new — I don't move until I understand why something works, not just that it does. In BJJ and boxing I'd rather be on the receiving end of a technique first, feel what it's supposed to do, before I try to throw it myself. In code review I'm the one asking why this approach and not another, even when the fix already works. Trading's the same itch: it's less about the trade and more about understanding the mechanism well enough to trust it.

That front-loaded curiosity is also why AI ended up central to how I build — not as a shortcut around understanding, but as the fastest way to get an idea into something tangible enough to interrogate.

Outside of all that: still working through a list of places I want to see in person before I theorize about them any further — pyramids, ruins, the stuff that's been sitting there for thousands of years not explaining itself.
